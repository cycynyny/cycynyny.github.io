Fundamental principles of heat transfer with engineering applications. Emphasis on analytical and numerical solution methods and design-oriented problem solving in thermal systems.

## Topics Covered

- Conduction: steady-state and transient, 1D and 2D
- Convection: internal and external flows, forced and natural
- Radiation: blackbody, gray surfaces, view factors
- Combined-mode heat transfer
- Heat exchangers: LMTD and NTU methods
- Fins and extended surfaces

## Course Details

- **Units:** 3 (1 lecture + 1 activity section)
- **Offered:** Every semester since Fall 2024
- **Prerequisites:** Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics
